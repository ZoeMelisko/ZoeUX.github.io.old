Overview

Tailoring personal experiences for luxury fashion e-commerce

The Challenge

Choisi is a Los Angeles based startup focused on creating an online fashion marketplace that connects fashion-conscious consumers to emerging international designers. What sets them apart? Choisi has created an AI feature that curates fashion discoveries based on users online shopping habits and style preferences.

Our Goals

Choisi currently serves a purpose as a lookbook that directs users to designer sites causing a bounce rate of 60%. Additionally, the company has had difficulty implementing their own AI technology because of limitations with their website hosting platform. Choisi's founder, Sherry Soliman, came to us with two goals: Decrease the site’s bounce rate by at least 10% and Create a personalized shopping experience on the current site that doesn't utilize AI
